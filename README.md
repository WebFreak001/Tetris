Controls
---

left - move left

right - move right

ctrl - rotate left

up - rotate right

shift - hold

space - hard drop

down - soft drop

### Alternative controls

Rename `controls_dkeio.json` to `controls.json` to get those, are much more fun in my opinion.

D - move left

K - move right

E - rotate left

I - rotate right

O - hold

space - hard drop

shift - soft drop
